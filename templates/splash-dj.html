Playlists for Runners

Build playlists full of energy to support your run.



Research shows that the right music can boost your mood and energy levels, but finding the perfect playlist can be a challenge.

Song2Run analyzes your existing playlist and help you curate the perfect running soundtrack tailored to your personal taste and training goals.
It also supports song ordering to help you build endurance, increase intensity, or mix it up with intervals.

Give a spin and power your run like never before.


Login to Spotify is needed to retrieve the songs you like from your playlists and to save the newly created playlists.
